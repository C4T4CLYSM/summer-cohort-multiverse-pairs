# HumboldtJoker

**What they're building**
Downbeat is a privacy-first VS Code extension that shows brief text ads in your status bar while your AI tool is "thinking," and pays you 50% of the ad revenue. 

**Who it's for:**
Developers who use AI coding tools (especially Claude Code) heavily enough that they rack up real idle "thinking" time each day and would take passive income for it. Likely audience seems it would be indie devs and privacy-conscious open-source folks.
